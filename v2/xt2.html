Carbon Footprint Calculator

Every time we turn on a light, drive to school, or throw something in the trash, we contribute to our carbon footprint.

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (like carbon dioxide and methane) released into the atmosphere because of our daily activities.

School events—whether a sports day, an annual function, or an art exhibition—also have a carbon footprint. The electricity used, food served or wasted, decorations, and so on all leave an environmental impact.

But what if your school could track and reduce that impact?

Here’s WWF-India’s innovative Carbon Footprint Calculator for Schools!

This easy-to-use digital tool helps schools to:

  • Plan a Carbon Conscious event: Enter details and get an estimate of the emissions your event might generate, along with simple tips to reduce the carbon footprint.
  • Measure the impact post-event: After your event, enter the actual data and see the emissions produced with insights for improvement.
  • Earn recognition as a Climate Champion School: Schools using the calculator to track and reduce their event emissions will be recognized by WWF-India.

Host a Carbon Conscious school event and join WWF-India’s League of Climate Champion Schools today!
