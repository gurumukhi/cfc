Carbon Footprint Calculator

Every time we turn on a light, drive to school, or throw something in the trash, we contribute to our carbon footprint.

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (like carbon dioxide and methane) released into the atmosphere because of our daily activities.

School events—whether a sports day, an annual function, or an art exhibition—also have a carbon footprint. The electricity used, food served or wasted, decoration used, and so on all leave an environmental impact.

But what if your school could track and reduce that impact?

Here’s WWF-India’s innovative Carbon Footprint Calculator for Schools!

This easy-to-use digital tool helps schools to:

  • Plan a Carbon Conscious event: When planning your event, enter details and get an estimate of the emissions your event might generate. Plus, you’ll get simple, practical suggestions on how to cut the carbon.
  • Measure the impact post-event: After your school event, enter actual data to calculate the emissions produced and get insights on how to do better next time!
  • Earn recognition as a Climate Champion School: Schools that actively use the calculator to track and reduce their emissions generated at school events will be recognised by WWF-India as Climate Champion Schools.

By using this unique tool, your school can take impactful steps toward environmental responsibility and be part of the global effort for climate action.
